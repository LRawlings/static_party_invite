The post code will not get you to the exact location. When you are getting close, please follow the home-made signs.

You're welcome to drive, there's plenty of parking. You will need to drive up the bumpy track and park in a field so please leave your Ferraris and Lamborghinis at home!

There is not much in the way of local Taxis so we have booked an 8 seater minibus to be on standby from 12:30 - 15:30 to take people from their accommodation to the venue and again at 22:00 - 01:00 to take people home

You will need to pay for mileage but we can guarantee availability.
